# Welcome to Tema_Q

This is the Tema_Q development team, managed by ek15072809, Yuna, Kawasumi and Amateur-Info-System. This organization focuses on developing and releasing projects related to Avant-garde and practical Large Language Models (LLMs).

This organization posts the flagship models of Tema_Q. Please check member posts for draft models, derivative models, and quantized models.

- [About our models](https://huggingface.co/spaces/temaq-org/temaq)

Tema_Q is designed to generate more open-ended and useful responses to prompts that are difficult to answer using standard models. See below for more information on the usefulness of creative LLMs.

- [The Importance of Tema_Q LLMs](https://note.com/tema_q/n/n69e761ee6c30)
